Laptops

Laptops are portable computers designed for convenience and mobility. They combine a screen, keyboard, touchpad (or trackpad), and internal hardware into a single compact unit. Laptops are commonly used for work, education, gaming, and entertainment. They come in various types, including ultrabooks (lightweight and slim), gaming laptops (high-performance), and 2-in-1 laptops (convertible or detachable for tablet mode). Key specifications to consider include the processor, RAM, storage type (SSD or HDD), screen size, battery life, and operating system (Windows, macOS, or Linux).
