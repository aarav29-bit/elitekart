Brass Material

The brass work of Moradabad, often called the **"Brass City of India,"** is renowned for its intricate craftsmanship and artistic excellence. Artisans create beautifully engraved, etched, and embossed brassware, including vases, trays, lamps, and decorative items. Combining traditional techniques with contemporary designs, Moradabad brassware is prized globally for its elegance and durability.
